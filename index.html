IVIS Project 2

Visualizing the World Through its Own Worldview

About

The parallel coordinates represent people's well being and what they believe is important in life on a scale from 1-4.

Filter: Click on item in list to filter.
Brush: Drag vertically along an axis.
Remove Brush: Tap the axis background.
Reorder Axes: Drag a label horizontally.
Invert Axis: Tap an axis label.
Remove Axis: Drag axis label to the left edge.

Adapted from example by Kai Chang
